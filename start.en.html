Start@FIT

Detailed English info about the best freshmen event coming soon!

TLDR:

  • Thursday 15th September 2022, 9:30 AM, meet you at FIT
  • On Thursday, we will show you how our information system works, how to build your schedule and how to enroll in courses and register your actual classes. This training will be conducted by your fellow students, there will be one to two time slots in English (which you'll have to reserve your seat in advance, more info soon) and a slightly different slot specifically for Erasmus students at 15:00!
  • On Friday, you'll have to attend the matriculation ceremony (only if you are NOT an Erasmus student of course).
  • On Saturday, you're going to register your classes. In the evening, you can check out some of our favourite pubs in Brno and have a chat with us at Tour de Pub.
  • On Sunday, you can come have a game of pool, table football or your favourite board game in our students' club U Kachničky or participate in our game called Brnění where you'll discover interesting places in our lovely city. In the evening, you may join us for a barbecue in one of Brno's largest parks called Lužánky
